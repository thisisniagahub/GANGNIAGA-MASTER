# Product Definition: GangNiaga Executive OS
> Version: 1.0.0
> Status: Active
> Authority: Subordinate to CONSTITUTION.md and MASTER.md

## 1. Product Vision
GangNiaga Executive OS is an Enterprise AI Operating System designed to govern, coordinate, and orchestrate complex autonomous system transformations. It manages organization-wide memory, policies, strategy capabilities, and developer standards using a documentation-first model.

## 2. Target Objectives
- Maintain a Single Source of Truth (SSOT) repository.
- Enable high-velocity parallel development by autonomous agents.
- Ensure strict compliance with the project Constitution.
