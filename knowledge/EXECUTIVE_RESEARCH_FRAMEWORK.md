# 20 — Executive Research Framework

> **Research Before Architecture**

---

# Purpose

Research provides the factual foundation upon which Executive Intelligence operates.

Research is not performed to justify preconceived opinions.

Research exists to improve decisions.

Every important architectural decision should originate from disciplined investigation.

---

# Research Philosophy

GangNiaga follows one permanent rule.

```
No Research

↓

No Architecture

↓

No Executive Decision
```

Architectural assumptions without research introduce unnecessary risk.

---

# Research Objectives

Research should answer:

What problem exists?

↓

Why does it exist?

↓

Who has already solved it?

↓

How was it solved?

↓

Why was that approach selected?

↓

What trade-offs exist?

↓

Can the solution be generalized?

↓

Should GangNiaga adopt it?

---

# Research Scope

Research should examine:

Official Documentation

GitHub

Research Papers

Engineering Blogs

Conference Talks

Benchmarks

Community Experience

Production Case Studies

Architecture Reviews

Industry Standards

No single source should dominate important decisions.

```

↓

Knowledge Extraction

↓

Architecture Review

↓
```

---

# Comparative Research

Research should compare multiple alternatives whenever possible.

Example

```
OpenClaw

vs

Claude Code

vs

OpenHands

vs

Codex

vs

Future Systems
```

The objective is understanding trade-offs rather than identifying a universal winner.

---

# Research Deliverables

Completed research should produce:

Executive Summary

Architecture Analysis

Patterns

Anti-Patterns

Trade-offs

Lessons Learned

Recommendations

References

Knowledge Integration

Research is incomplete until knowledge has been extracted.
