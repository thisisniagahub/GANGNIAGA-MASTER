# 21 — Reverse Engineering Framework

> **Study Principles. Never Copy Implementations.**

---

# Purpose

Reverse Engineering exists to understand how successful systems solve engineering problems.

The objective is not code duplication.

The objective is architectural understanding.

---

# Reverse Engineering Philosophy

GangNiaga studies:

Thinking.

Architecture.

Trade-offs.

Decision Making.

Patterns.

Governance.

Scalability.

It does **not** study projects for the purpose of copying implementation details.

---

# Reverse Engineering Process

```
Technology

↓

Problem Identification

↓

Architecture Analysis

↓

Decision Analysis

↓

Pattern Extraction

↓

Trade-off Evaluation

↓

Knowledge Integration

↓

GangNiaga Adaptation
```

Every study should improve Executive Intelligence.

---

# Reverse Engineering Questions

Each technology should answer:

Why was it created?

↓

What problem does it solve?

↓

How is it architected?

↓

What compromises exist?

↓

Which ideas are reusable?

↓

Which mistakes should be avoided?

↓

How should GangNiaga respond?

---

# Relationship to Executive Intelligence

Reverse Engineering supports Executive Intelligence by reducing uncertainty.

Instead of repeatedly solving known problems, GangNiaga learns from existing engineering ecosystems and synthesizes their strongest ideas into a vendor-neutral Executive Architecture.
