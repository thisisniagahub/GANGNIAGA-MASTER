# 26 — Template System

> **Standardized Knowledge Creation**

---

# Purpose

Templates ensure that knowledge is created consistently across the entire repository.

Templates reduce ambiguity.

They improve documentation quality.

They accelerate onboarding.

They allow AI Agents to produce predictable outputs.

---

# Philosophy

Templates are not forms.

Templates are knowledge frameworks.

Their objective is to ensure that every important engineering activity captures the same critical information.
