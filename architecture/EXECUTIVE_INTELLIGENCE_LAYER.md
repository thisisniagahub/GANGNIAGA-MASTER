# 11 — Executive Intelligence Layer

## Purpose

The Executive Intelligence Layer is the brain of GangNiaga Executive OS.

Every strategic activity begins here.

It does **not** execute code.

It does **not** call AI models directly.

Instead, it coordinates everything beneath it.

---

## Responsibilities

Executive Intelligence is responsible for:

- Understanding objectives.
- Identifying problems.
- Evaluating constraints.
- Defining priorities.
- Coordinating architecture.
- Managing executive context.
- Delegating capabilities.
- Preserving organizational knowledge.

Executive Intelligence decides.

Other layers execute.

---

## Inputs

Executive Intelligence receives information from:

- User Requests
- Business Goals
- Organizational Knowledge
- Research
- ADR
- Previous Decisions
- Memory
- External Knowledge

---

## Outputs

Executive Intelligence produces:

- Executive Objectives
- Planning Instructions
- Architectural Direction
- Capability Requirements
- Decision Requests
- Governance Policies

These outputs become inputs for lower layers.

---

# Executive Intelligence Responsibilities

Executive Intelligence continuously answers questions such as:

```
What is the objective?

↓

What knowledge already exists?

↓

What architecture supports this?

↓

What capabilities are required?

↓

What risks exist?

↓

What should happen next?
```

This reasoning process defines Executive Thinking.

---

# Executive Context

Executive Intelligence always operates using complete context.

Context includes:

- Vision
- Mission
- Philosophy
- Principles
- ADR
- Architecture
- Research
- Repository Standards
- Previous Decisions
- Organizational Memory

No executive decision should occur without context.

---

↓

↓

↓

↓

Infrastructure Control

↓

Low-Level Execution

These belong elsewhere.
