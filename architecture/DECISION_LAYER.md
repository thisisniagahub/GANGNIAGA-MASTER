# 13 — Decision Layer

## Purpose

The Decision Layer transforms knowledge into engineering decisions.

Knowledge alone has limited value.

Value emerges when knowledge influences decisions.

---

# Decision Workflow

```
Problem

↓

Knowledge

↓

Alternatives

↓

Trade-offs

↓

Executive Review

↓

Decision

↓

ADR

↓

Architecture

↓

Execution
```
