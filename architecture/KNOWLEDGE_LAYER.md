# 12 — Knowledge Layer

## Purpose

The Knowledge Layer stores, organizes, retrieves, and continuously improves organizational knowledge.

Knowledge is treated as the primary asset of GangNiaga Executive OS.

Every engineering activity either:

Consumes knowledge,

or

Produces knowledge.
