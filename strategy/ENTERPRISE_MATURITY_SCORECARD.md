# Enterprise Maturity Scorecard

> **Document ID:** `GN-G8-004-MATURITY-SCORECARD`
> **Document type:** Scorecard
> **Status:** Draft
> **Owner:** Maturity Assessment Lead
> **Approver:** Programme Board

## 1. Objective
To assess current capability gaps across all domains using the established M0-M5 maturity scale.

## 2. Maturity Scale Definition
- **M0:** Absent
- **M1:** Documented
- **M2:** Approved and owned
- **M3:** Implemented and repeatable
- **M4:** Measured and independently assured
- **M5:** Continuously optimized

## 3. Domain Scorecard

| Domain | Current Maturity | Target Maturity | Gap | Remediation Owner | Evidence |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Business | M2 | M3 | Implement use cases | Strategy Lead | Business Capability Map |
| Governance | M3 | M4 | Automate assurance | Governance Lead | GRC Operating Model |
| Architecture | M3 | M4 | End-to-end tracing | Enterprise Architect | Target Architecture |
| Data | M2 | M3 | Lineage automation | Data Lead | Data Governance Policy |
| AI | M1 | M3 | Evaluation framework | AI Governance Lead | AI Inventory |
| Security | M3 | M4 | Threat automation | Security Architect | Security Architecture |
| Engineering | M3 | M4 | SDLC tooling | Engineering Lead | Secure SDLC |
| Operations | M3 | M4 | FinOps reporting | SRE Lead | SRE Service Model |
| Assurance | M2 | M4 | Dashboard build | Assurance Lead | Test Strategy |

## 4. Remediation Plan
Remediation items for all M1 and M2 capabilities have been logged in the continuous improvement backlog, assigned to their respective owners, and targeted for M3 within the next two quarters.
