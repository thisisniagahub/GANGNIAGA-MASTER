## 1. Strategy-on-a-Page

**North Star Vision:** 
To become the enterprise Executive Intelligence Layer that enables better decisions before execution, coordinates replaceable AI and execution capabilities, preserves organizational knowledge, and produces traceable business outcomes under explicit human authority.

**Mission (Phase 1):** 
Build a Company Knowledge & Executive Intelligence Foundation that connects business intent, enterprise knowledge, decision authority, AI capabilities, and execution systems in a controlled, risk-aware environment.

**Strategic Pillars:**
1. **Better Decision Quality:** Surface relevant evidence before commitment; make material decisions explainable and reviewable.
2. **Faster Responsible Execution:** Reuse approved patterns, automate low-risk work within boundaries, and reduce repeated discovery.
3. **Lower Enterprise Risk:** Preserve traceability, enforce human approval for consequential actions, and proactively detect unsafe executions.
4. **Durable Organizational Knowledge:** Preserve sources, decisions, outcomes, and lessons in a continuous feedback loop without confusing generated text with verified truth.

## 2. Outcome/KPI Tree

### Pillar 1: Better Decision Quality

#### Objective 1.1: Increase traceability of executive decisions to verified evidence.
- **Metric:** Percentage of material architectural and investment decisions (ADRs/Approvals) linking to verified knowledge/evidence.
- **Baseline:** 0% (Phase 0)
- **Target:** 100%
- **Owner:** Architecture Governance Lead
- **Data Source:** ADR Register / Approval Logs
- **Cadence:** Monthly
- **Review Date:** 2026-10-15

#### Objective 1.2: Reduce decision latency for standard architectural approvals.
- **Metric:** Average days from proposal to decision for standard (D2) changes.
- **Baseline:** 14 days (Estimated current state)
- **Target:** < 5 days
- **Owner:** Architecture Review Board Chair
- **Data Source:** Governance Issue Tracker
- **Cadence:** Quarterly
- **Review Date:** 2026-10-15
