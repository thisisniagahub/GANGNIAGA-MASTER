# Enterprise Operating Model

**Document ID:** `GN-STRAT-OM-001`
**Status:** Approved
**Owner:** Operating Model Lead
**Approver:** Executive Sponsor

## 1. Principles
Every planned service in the GangNiaga ecosystem must have explicitly defined ownership across all lifecycle dimensions. Responsibility gaps equal zero.

## 2. Service Ownership Matrix

| Service / Platform | Business Owner | Product Owner | Technical Owner | Data Owner | Control Owner | Support Owner |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Executive Intelligence Core** | CEO | VP Product | Chief Architect | CDO | CISO | L3 Support |
| **Governance Engine** | PMO Director | Gov. Prod Mgr | Lead Engineer | Info Gov Lead | Risk Officer | Ops Team |
| **Knowledge Graph** | Chief Strategy | Knowledge Mgr | Data Architect | CDO | Privacy Lead | Data Ops |
| **Zero-Dependency Preview** | VP Engineering | DevEx Manager | Staff Engineer | Tech Lead | SecOps | Dev Support |

## 3. Escalation & Lifecycle
- **Escalation Path:** Support Owner -> Technical Owner -> Product Owner -> Business Owner.
- **Review Cadence:** Ownership is reviewed quarterly and updated upon personnel changes.
