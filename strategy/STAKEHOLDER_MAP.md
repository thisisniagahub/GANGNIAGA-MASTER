## 1. Executive Summary
This document establishes the stakeholder and executive accountability map for the GangNiaga Enterprise Transformation. It satisfies the requirements of **GN-G1-001**, ensuring that 100% of identified stakeholder groups have defined engagement and decision roles, and that all critical capabilities have accountable business owners.

## 2. Stakeholder Categories and Accountability

### 2.1 Executives and Sponsorship
* **Executive Sponsor**
  * **Accountability:** Ultimate authority, funding provision, and go/no-go decisions for R4 risks.
  * **Engagement Role:** Approves strategic direction, business value roadmap, and risk appetite.
* **Executive Committee / Portfolio Board**
  * **Accountability:** Cross-functional alignment and portfolio governance.
  * **Engagement Role:** Reviews transformation progress and resolves cross-domain blockers.

### 2.2 Business Owners
* **Business Capability Owners**
  * **Accountability:** Realization of specific business outcomes and benefits from platform capabilities.
  * **Engagement Role:** Defines capability requirements and accepts delivered solutions.
* **Product Lead**
  * **Accountability:** Use-case portfolio prioritization and platform product-market fit.
  * **Engagement Role:** Bridges business outcomes with technology execution.

### 2.3 Technology and Platform
* **Chief/Lead Architect**
  * **Accountability:** Enterprise architecture, technical standards, and target-state integrity.
  * **Engagement Role:** Chairs Architecture Review Board; governs ADRs.
* **Platform Engineering Lead**
  * **Accountability:** Environment stability, deployment pipelines, and SDLC controls.
  * **Engagement Role:** Ensures production readiness and supply chain security.

### 2.4 Data, AI, and Knowledge
* **Chief Data Officer / Data Governance Lead**
  * **Accountability:** Enterprise data domains, quality, classification, and lifecycle.
  * **Engagement Role:** Chairs Data Governance Council.
* **Knowledge Governance Lead**
  * **Accountability:** Validity, curation, and lifecycle of the enterprise knowledge base.
  * **Engagement Role:** Governs knowledge provenance and decay.
* **AI Governance Lead**
  * **Accountability:** AI inventory, model risk, and evaluation frameworks.
  * **Engagement Role:** Enforces responsible-AI controls and human oversight mechanisms.

### 2.5 Security and Privacy
* **CISO / Security Authority**
  * **Accountability:** Enterprise security posture, threat modeling, and residual risk acceptance.
  * **Engagement Role:** Approves identity controls, encryption, and incident response readiness.
* **Privacy Engineering Lead**
  * **Accountability:** Privacy-by-design, data minimization, and cross-border compliance.
  * **Engagement Role:** Reviews DPIAs and integrates privacy controls into SDLC.

### 2.6 Legal, Compliance, and Audit
* **Legal Counsel**
  * **Accountability:** IP protection, contracts, open-source policy, and legal holds.
  * **Engagement Role:** Translates legal obligations into platform requirements.
* **Compliance Lead**
  * **Accountability:** Adherence to external regulatory and policy frameworks.
  * **Engagement Role:** Maintains the obligation register (GN-G2-003).
* **Assurance / Internal Audit Lead**
  * **Accountability:** Independent verification of controls and maturity.
  * **Engagement Role:** Conducts periodic audits; reports on residual risks.

### 2.7 Finance and Procurement
* **FinOps Lead / Finance Owner**
  * **Accountability:** Budget adherence, cost allocation, and ROI tracking.
  * **Engagement Role:** Establishes cost-awareness controls and performance budgets.
* **Procurement / Vendor Risk Lead**
  * **Accountability:** Third-party supplier risk and contract lifecycle.
  * **Engagement Role:** Conducts due diligence and maintains the supplier inventory.

### 2.8 Operations and Support
* **SRE / Service Management Lead**
  * **Accountability:** Service reliability, incident response, and SLI/SLO adherence.
  * **Engagement Role:** Owns runbooks, on-call schedules, and disaster recovery execution.
* **AI Operations Lead**
  * **Accountability:** Monitoring AI models for drift, safety, and performance.
  * **Engagement Role:** Manages AI rollback and decommissioning processes.

### 2.9 Employees and Users
* **End Users (Employees)**
  * **Accountability:** Responsible usage within established policies.
  * **Engagement Role:** Provides feedback and user-acceptance testing.
* **Change Management Lead**
  * **Accountability:** User adoption and organizational readiness.
  * **Engagement Role:** Designs training programs and communication plans.
